# Overview

Each country has their own Meteorological Organizations:

- **USA**:
	- National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)

- **Austria:**
   - Zentralanstalt für Meteorologie und Geodynamik (ZAMG) - Central Institute for Meteorology and Geodynamics

- **Belgium:**
   - Royal Meteorological Institute (RMI/IRM) - Koninklijk Meteorologisch Instituut

- **Bulgaria:**
   - National Institute of Meteorology and Hydrology (NIMH) - Национален институт по метеорология и хидрология

- **Croatia:**
   - State Hydrometeorological Institute (DHMZ) - Državni hidrometeorološki zavod

- **Cyprus:**
   - Department of Meteorology - Τμήμα Μετεωρολογίας

- **Czech Republic:**
   - Czech Hydrometeorological Institute (CHMI) - Český hydrometeorologický ústav

- **Denmark:**
   - Danish Meteorological Institute (DMI) - Danmarks Meteorologiske Institut

- **Estonia:**
   - Estonian Weather Service - Ilmateenistus

- **Finland:**
   - Finnish Meteorological Institute (FMI) - Ilmatieteen laitos

- **France:**
    - Météo-France - Météo-France

- **Germany:**
    - Deutscher Wetterdienst (DWD) - German Weather Service

- **Greece:**
    - Hellenic National Meteorological Service (HNMS) - Εθνική Μετεωρολογική Υπηρεσία

- **Hungary:**
    - Hungarian Meteorological Service (OMSZ) - Országos Meteorológiai Szolgálat

- **Ireland:**
    - Met Éireann - The Irish Meteorological Service

- **Italy:**
    - Servizio Meteorologico - Italian Air Force Meteorological Service

- **Latvia:**
    - Latvian Environment, Geology and Meteorology Centre (LVĢMC) - Vides, ģeoloģijas un meteoroloģijas centrs

- **Lithuania:**
    - Lithuanian Hydrometeorological Service (LHMT) - Lietuvos hidrometeorologijos tarnyba

- **Luxembourg:**
    - Administration de l'Environnement - Direction de la météorologie

- **Malta:**
    - Malta Weather - Servizz tal-Meteorologija

- **Netherlands:**
    - Royal Netherlands Meteorological Institute (KNMI) - Koninklijk Nederlands Meteorologisch Instituut

- **Poland:**
    - Institute of Meteorology and Water Management - National Research Institute (IMGW-PIB) - Instytut Meteorologii i Gospodarki Wodnej - Państwowy Instytut Badawczy

- **Portugal:**
    - Instituto Português do Mar e da Atmosfera (IPMA) - Portuguese Institute for the Sea and Atmosphere

- **Romania:**
    - National Meteorological Administration (ANM) - Administrația Națională de Meteorologie

- **Slovakia:**
    - Slovak Hydrometeorological Institute (SHMÚ) - Slovenský hydrometeorologický ústav

- **Slovenia:**
    - Environmental Agency of the Republic of Slovenia - Agencija Republike Slovenije za okolje

- **Spain:**
    - State Meteorological Agency (AEMET) - Agencia Estatal de Meteorología

- **Sweden:**
    - Swedish Meteorological and Hydrological Institute (SMHI) - Sveriges meteorologiska och hydrologiska institut

- **Cyprus:**
    - Met Office Cyprus - Μετεωρολογικό Τμήμα Κύπρου
